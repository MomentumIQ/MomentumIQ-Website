About MomentumIQ

MomentumIQ builds AI-powered financial tools using Federated Learning — delivering powerful personalization without storing user data in the cloud or requiring large cloud compute.

Our Technology

Everything we build is grounded in Federated Learning — a technique that trains AI models directly on users' devices rather than centralizing data on a server.

Federated Learning

Models are trained locally on each device and aggregated without ever exposing raw user data.

Zero Cloud Storage

We don't store personal financial data. Your information stays on your device, always.
