Our Technology

We build on Federated Learning — a technique that trains AI models directly on users' devices rather than centralizing data on a server. It's how we deliver personalization without compromising privacy.

Federated Learning

Models are trained locally on each device and aggregated without ever exposing raw user data.

Zero Cloud Storage

We don't store personal financial data. Your information stays on your device, always.

Privacy by Design

Our products are built so your data is never a liability — for you or for us. On-device for consumers. Zero-custody execution for investors.
